Why This Matters

You thought your G&A was 12%. It was actually 15%. That 3-point difference costs $50,000.

On a $2M fixed price contract with 60% direct labor, a G&A rate that's 3 points higher than you thought pushes your wrap rate from 1.50 to 1.54 — and $50,000 in unrecovered indirect costs comes straight out of your profit before you deliver a single hour of work.
